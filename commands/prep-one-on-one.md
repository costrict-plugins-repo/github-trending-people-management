# /prep-one-on-one
Prepare a 1:1 that's worth their time.
## Steps
1. **Set the frame** — Plan a report-led agenda using `one-on-one` skill.
2. **Ready any feedback** — Draft specific feedback using `feedback-delivery` skill.
3. **Connect to growth** — Bring their development forward using `growth-plan` skill.
## Output
A 1:1 prep note: their likely agenda, any feedback to give, and a growth thread to pull on.
Consider following up with `/run-review-cycle` when the review period approaches.
